Answer key
The Cell Lab Breakout. All 8 rooms.

Final code: GENETICS
Letter from each room in order: G, E, N, E, T, I, C, S

Room 1, Chemistry of Life
Sort the 4 molecules:
Glucose: carbohydrate
Triglyceride: lipid
Keratin: protein
mRNA: nucleic acid

Last question: What is the polysaccharide that animals use to store glucose in the liver and muscle?
Answer: glycogen. Letter: G.

Room 2, Cell Structure
Click the following organelles in order: nucleus, ribosome, rough ER, Golgi, vesicle. Mitochondrion, lysosome and chloroplast are redirections and will restart the process.

Last question: What is the abbreviation of the first organelle the polypeptide enters?
Answer: ER (or RER). Letter: E.

Room 3, Cellular Energetics
ATP per glucose:
Glycolysis: 2
Krebs: 2
ETC: about 28 (the game allows anything between 26 and 34)
Total: about 32

Final question: The primary electron donor to the ETC?
Answer: NADH. Letter: N.

Room 4, Cell Communication
GPCR cascade in order:
GPCR is bound by epinephrine.
Receptor activates a G-protein.
G-protein turns on adenylyl cyclase, which produces cAMP.
cAMP activates PKA.
PKA promotes breakdown of glycogen to make glucose.

Last question: General name for a catalyst?
Answer: enzyme. Letter: E.

Room 5, Heredity
Punnett for Pp x pp:
Genotypes of the offspring: Pp, Pp, pp, pp
Phenotype ratio: 1:1

Last question: Testcross?
Answer: testcross. Letter: T.

Room 6, Gene Expression
The pre-mRNA has 5 exons and 3 introns. All introns begin with GU and end with AG. Click to remove them.
Preserve: AUG, GAU, AAU, GCA, UAA
Remove: GUAAAG, GUACAG, GUAUAG
mRNA: AUG GAU AAU GCA UAA
Amino acids: Met, Asp, Asn, Ala, STOP. The middle three single-letter amino acids are like DNA.

Last question: What are the introns of a pre-mRNA?
Answer: introns. Letter: I.

Room 7, Natural Selection
The trait table is cumulative. Lancelet has no traits, lamprey has vertebrae, salamander has 4 limbs, lizard has the amniotic egg, mouse has hair. The correct tree branches (nests) each new species within the previous group.
Correct answer: Tree A.

Final question: Tree showing evolutionary relationships based on shared derived traits?
Answer: cladogram. Letter: C.

Room 8, Ecology
Order of primary succession:
Bare rock after the eruption
Lichens and mosses, soil begins to form
Grasses and shrubs
Mature forest (climax)

Last question: The progressive change in community leading to a climax community is ecological…
Answer: succession. Letter: S.

Final door
The letters spell Genetics. When they get it right they are presented with a certificate with their time and number of hints used.
