Answer key
The Cell Lab Breakout. All 8 rooms.

Final code: GENETICS
Letter from each room in order: G, E, N, E, T, I, C, S


Room 1, Chemistry of Life
Sort the 4 molecules:
- Glucose: carbohydrate
- Triglyceride: lipid
- Keratin: protein
- mRNA: nucleic acid

Final question: Which polysaccharide do animals store glucose as in liver and muscle?
Answer: glycogen. Letter: G.


Room 2, Cell Structure
Click the organelles in this order: nucleus, ribosome, rough ER, Golgi, vesicle. Mitochondrion, lysosome, and chloroplast are decoys and reset the path if you click them.

Final question: Type the abbreviation of the organelle the polypeptide enters first.
Answer: ER (or RER). Letter: E.


Room 3, Cellular Energetics
ATP per glucose:
- Glycolysis: 2
- Krebs: 2
- ETC: about 28 (the game accepts anything from 26 to 34)
- Total: about 32

Final question: The main electron carrier feeding the ETC?
Answer: NADH. Letter: N.


Room 4, Cell Communication
GPCR cascade in order:
1. Epinephrine binds the GPCR.
2. Receptor activates a G-protein.
3. G-protein activates adenylyl cyclase, which makes cAMP.
4. cAMP activates protein kinase A.
5. PKA triggers glycogen breakdown into glucose.

Final question: General term for a biological catalyst?
Answer: enzyme. Letter: E.


Room 5, Heredity
Punnett for Pp x pp:
- Offspring genotypes: Pp, Pp, pp, pp
- Phenotype ratio: 1:1

Final question: Cross of a dominant phenotype with a homozygous recessive?
Answer: testcross. Letter: T.


Room 6, Gene Expression
Pre-mRNA has 5 exons and 3 introns. The introns all start with GU and end with AG. Click them to splice them out.
- Keep: AUG, GAU, AAU, GCA, UAA
- Remove: GUAAAG, GUACAG, GUAUAG
- Mature mRNA: AUG GAU AAU GCA UAA
- Amino acids: Met, Asp, Asn, Ala, STOP. The middle three single-letter codes spell DNA.

Final question: What are the non-coding parts of a pre-mRNA called?
Answer: introns. Letter: I.


Room 7, Natural Selection
The trait table is cumulative. Lancelet has no derived traits, lamprey adds vertebrae, salamander adds 4 limbs, lizard adds the amniotic egg, mouse adds hair. The right tree nests each new species into the previous clade.
Correct answer: Tree A.

Final question: Diagram showing evolutionary relationships based on shared derived traits?
Answer: cladogram. Letter: C.


Room 8, Ecology
Order of primary succession:
1. Bare rock after the eruption
2. Lichens and mosses move in, soil starts forming
3. Grasses and shrubs
4. Mature forest (climax)

Final question: Gradual community change ending in a climax community is ecological...
Answer: succession. Letter: S.


Final door
The letters in order spell GENETICS. After typing it correctly the player sees a certificate with their time and hint count.
