Welcome to the Cell Lab.

You have been locked inside Dr. Krebs' biology lab overnight, and the AP Exam begins at sunrise. Between you and the door are eight containment rooms, one for each unit of AP Biology, and every room hides a single letter that fits into the final escape code, so if you solve every room and put the letters together in the order you collected them, you can finally leave.

How to play: you will move through the eight rooms in order, and each one asks you to work through a short puzzle built around a specific unit of AP Biology. When you solve it correctly the room reveals a single letter, and that letter becomes part of the eight-letter escape code you type at the very end. You cannot skip ahead to the next room, but you can always tap the hint button if you get stuck, and since there is no timer running you can play solo or with a small group at your own pace.
