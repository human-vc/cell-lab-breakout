The Cell Lab Breakout
An AP Bio review game. Runs in any browser.


The setup
You're locked in Dr. Krebs' bio lab the night before the AP exam. Eight rooms stand between you and the door, one for each unit. Each room hides a letter. Get all 8 letters, spell the word, escape.


How to play
Open index.html in Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge. Click "Enter Room 1" on the welcome screen. Each room is a short puzzle, and once you solve it a final question shows up that gives you the letter for that room. After Room 8 you type all 8 letters into the final lock.

The bar at the top of the screen shows which rooms you've cleared. There's a hint button on every room if you get stuck. Hints don't penalize you, they just get counted on the certificate at the end.


The rooms
1. Chemistry of Life: drag 4 molecules into the right macromolecule bins.
2. Cell Structure: trace a secreted protein by clicking organelles in order.
3. Cellular Energetics: fill in the ATP yields for glycolysis, Krebs, and the ETC.
4. Cell Communication: drag the GPCR cascade steps into the right order.
5. Heredity: fill in a Punnett square for Pp x pp and pick the right ratio.
6. Gene Expression: click the introns out of a pre-mRNA.
7. Natural Selection: read a trait table and pick the right cladogram.
8. Ecology: drag the stages of primary succession into order.


Notes
There's no timer. The game tracks your total time and the number of hints you used, and both show up on the certificate so you can replay and try to beat your run. Hints are unlimited. Once it loads, the whole thing runs offline.


For class use
Easiest setup is to drop index.html in Canvas or Drive and let everyone open it on their own laptop, Chromebook, or phone. Group stations work too, or you can project it and play as a class.

When you finish you get a certificate with your time and hint count. Print it or screenshot it.
