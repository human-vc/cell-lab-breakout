The Cell Lab Breakout
An AP Bio review game. Runs in any browser.

The setup
It's the night before the AP test, and you're locked in Dr. Krebs' biology lab. There are 8 rooms between you and freedom, one for each unit. Each room is a letter. Find the 8 letters, use them, escape.

How to play
Load index.html in Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge. Click on "Enter Room 1" on the main page. All rooms are a mini-puzzle, and once complete, a final question appears to give you the room's letter. When you're done Room 8, you enter the 8 letters into the lock.

On the top there's a bar telling you which rooms you have done. You can click the hint button on each room. They don't cost you points, just count on your certificate.

The rooms
Chemistry of Life: place 4 molecules into the correct macromolecule bins.
Cell Structure: click the organelles in the order needed for a secreted protein.
Cellular Energetics: drag the numbers of ATP made by glycolysis, Krebs, and the ETC.
Cell Communication: put the GPCR cascade in the correct sequence.
Heredity: fill in a Punnett square for Pp x pp and pick the right ratio.
Gene Expression: cut the introns out of a pre-mRNA.
Natural Selection: fill in a trait table and select the correct cladogram.
Ecology: click the stages of primary succession out of order.

Notes
There's no timer. It does keep track of your time and how many hints you use, and it displays them on the certificate so you can play again and improve your time. Hints are unlimited. Once the game loads, it's all offline.

For class use
Simplest way to use is to put index.html into Canvas or Drive and have students open it in their laptop, Chromebook or phone. You can play at stations, or project and play together.

At the end it gives you a certificate with your time and hints used. Print it or take a screenshot.
